About Us

First Step Lawn Mowing is a teen-run lawn care business based in Rapid City, South Dakota. We're young, motivated, and ready to work hard to keep your yard looking great.

We value great communication, strong work ethic, and—most of all—pleased customers. Whether it's a quick mow or a full clean-up, we treat every job like it's our own lawn.

We're just getting started, but we’re committed to making a big impact in our neighborhood one lawn at a time.

Thanks for supporting local youth and giving us the opportunity to grow!
