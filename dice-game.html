How to Play:
- Start with a balance of $100.
- Bet up to 95% of balance using the slider.
- Dealer rolls first. Dealer must hit until 17 or more.
- Then you roll. Try to reach 21 without going over.
- After rolling, choose to “Hit” or “Stand”.
- If your total is higher and ≤ 21, you win!
