How to Play
How to Play:
- Start with a balance of $100.
- Place a bet using the slider (max 95% of balance).
- Roll your dice. Goal is to get close to 21 without going over.
- After rolling, you can "Hit" to roll one more die or "Stand" to stop.
- Dealer rolls to try to beat your total (without going over 21).
- If your total is higher or the dealer busts, you win!
- Restart anytime with the button in the bottom-right.
