How to Play: (click to expand)
- Start with a balance of $100.
- Place a bet using the slider (max 95% of balance).
- Dealer rolls first.
- Your goal is to get close to 21 without going over.
- Roll your dice after dealer reveals theirs.
- You may "Hit" for more dice or "Stand".
- If your total is higher and ≤ 21, or dealer busts, you win!
- Dealer stands if their total is 17 or more.
- Restart anytime using the button in the bottom-right.
